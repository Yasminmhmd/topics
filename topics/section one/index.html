my story

In a quiet village, an old clock tower stood silent, forgotten by time. One night, a curious girl named Elara wandered near it, drawn by its mystery.
As she touched the ancient wood, the clock chimed, awakening the spirits of lost dreams. Whispers filled the air, weaving stories of love and longing. Elara listened, captivated, as they shared tales of hope, reminding her of her own unrealized dreams . With newfound courage, she vowed to chase them. The next morning, the clock began to tick again, and the village felt alive with possibility.


         Elara painted her dreams across the sky, inspiring others to do the same.
          The clock tower became a symbol of rebirth, reminding everyone to embrace their dreams. 
         And from that day on, time never felt so precious.
